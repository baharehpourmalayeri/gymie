Our Story

Founded in 2023, Moveo has been driven by one goal: turning every movement into enjoyment and strength.

Moveo brings together expert-designed workouts, community challenges, and an engaging platform to make fitness something you look forward to every day. Our goal is to help you move at your own pace, celebrate progress, and enjoy the journey.

We’re not just about exercise, we’re about creating a space where fitness feels exciting, achievable, and part of everyday life. Join us and discover how fun and rewarding staying active can be!
